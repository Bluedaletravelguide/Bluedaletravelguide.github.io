    Jalan Alor

    Hosting a number of hawker stalls and seafood restaurants, Jalan Alor is one of the most popular food streets in Kuala Lumpur. Located just behind Jalan Bukit Bintang and a short walk away from Changkat Bukit Bintang, this is a favourite dining spot in the Golden Triangle. A sharp contrast to trendy Jalan Bukit Bintang and Changkat Bukit Bintang, Jalan Alor's street food is available every day, known as KL 's food haven for most of us. There's so much to eat that you're not going to be able to finish in just one day.

    Location:
    Bukit Bintang, Kuala Lumpur, Federal Territory of Kuala Lumpur

    Taman Connaught Night Market

    Taman Connaught Night Market is very popular with the local community for its vast array of Malaysian hawker dishes. There are also a variety of food stalls selling Korean, Thai, Taiwanese and Japanese snacks at affordable prices. Located along Jalan Cerdas, the Taman Connaught Night Market is accessible by MRT from Kuala Lumpur.

    Location:
    130, 108, Jalan Cerdas, Taman Connaught, 56000 Kuala Lumpur, Federal Territory of Kuala Lumpur
    Operating Hours:
    6.00 pm - 1.00 am (Wed)

    Petaling Street

    While Petaling Street is well known one of the shopping paradises in Kuala Lumpur, it is also known for the hawker food. It still houses some of the oldest street-food stalls in Malaysia. If you want to enjoy the classic flavours of this country's diverse cuisine head there before the peak hours (or after, which ver rocks your boat).

    Location:
    Jalan Petaling, City Centre, 50000 Kuala Lumpur, Wilayah Persekutuan Kuala Lumpur
    Operating Hours:
    Open 24 Hours (Daily)

    Tapak Urban Street Dining

    Located close KLCC and the Golden Triangle, you'll find everything from coconut shakes to soft-shell crab burgers. Take the time to explore the 30 + food trucks stationed here. Gather your best buds and grab a meal on the wheels to enjoy some of KL's most creative food company under the stars.

    Location:
    Persiaran Hampshire, Off, Jalan Ampang, 50450 Kuala Lumpur, Federal Territory of Kuala Lumpur
    Operating Hours:
    6.00 pm - 12.00 am (Daily) Website:
    Persiaran Hampshire, Off, Jalan Ampang, 50450 Kuala Lumpur, Federal Territory of Kuala Lumpur

    Hutong Lot 10

    Lot 10 Hutong is a gourmet heritage village with some of the country's best eateries that have been passed down over the generations. The minimum requirement for every brand to be at Lot 10 Hutong is a track record of at least 40 years and an instantly recognisable brand name.

    Location:
    LG Floor, Lot 10 Shopping Centre, 50, Jalan Sultan Ismail, 50250 Kuala Lumpur, Federal Territory of Kuala Lumpur
    Operating Hours:
    10.00 am - 10.00 pm (Daily)
    Website:
