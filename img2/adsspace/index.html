ABOUT US

Bluedale Publishing proudly brings to you KL The Guide! We are a Free Travel related guide who delivers valuable tips and information on the whereabouts of your travels around the stunning city of Kuala Lumpur.

We offer services to foreigners and locals alike, providing the most up to dates information and trends of the most popular places to visit. Our guide covers all the different types of interest people have in mind when visiting KL from the most popular places to sightsee and visit to places with excellent ratings to eat and shop.
