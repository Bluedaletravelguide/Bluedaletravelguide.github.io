    Spas in KL

    Ozmosis Health & Day Spa

    Ozmosis Fitness & Day Spa is perfect for men and women looking to relax their bodies, minds, and souls. It is a serene haven situated right in the heart of Bangsar Baru and follows the idea of a Balinese spa where tranquilly prevails and all five senses are awakened and refreshed.

    Location: 16-1, Jalan Telawi 2, Bangsar, 59100 Kuala Lumpur
    Operating Hours: 10.00am-9.00pm (Monday-Sunday)
    Phone: +603-2287 0380

    Lavish Spa

    Nestled in the heart of Kuala Lumpur within Fahrenheit 88, Lavish Spa offers a holistic sanctuary for those eager to escape the tedium of daily life. Customers can take a dip in the outdoor swimming pool, relax in the jacuzzi, or work out an invigorating workout in a fully equipped gym, before indulging in the talents of experienced therapists for a relaxing massage or a mani-pedi service. Other facilities offered include a movie theatre, a lounge room, and a cafe serving international cuisine to soak up appetites between sampling sessions.

    Location: Lot 5-02-06, 179, Jalan Gading, Bukit Bintang, 55100 Kuala Lumpur
    Operating Hours: Open for 24 hours
    Phone: +603-2148 5888

    Swasana Spa

    Swasana Spa offers a range of seven body massages in the form of traditional Balinese, Thai and Malayan treatments. All massages are built to offer guests the ultimate rejuvenation and indulgence. Swasana Spa has been accredited as a 5-star resort by the Ministry of Tourism of Malaysia, the recipient of the World Luxury Spa Award and several other awards.

    Location: Impiana @ Klcc Hotel, No. 13, Jalan Pinang, Kuala Lumpur, 50450 Kuala Lumpur
    Operating Hours: 10.00 am-10.00 pm (Monday-Sunday)
    Phone: 03-2147 1111

    Alam Beauty and Wellness Spa

    After all that walking around KL, why not indulge yourself in a quick pamper at Alam Beauty and Wellness Spa? Located within Anggun Hotel, the spa offers a wide range of massage treatments along with facials and manicures. All you need to do is choose the treatment that best suits your needs and let Alam's professional masseurs and masseuses take care of you. You’ll surely wake up feeling refreshed and ready to conquer Kuala Lumpur!

    Location: 9, Tengkat Tong Shin, Bukit Bintang, 55100 Kuala Lumpur.
    Phone: +6019-212 0639

    Uroot Spa

    The Uroot Spa is one of the best massage spas in the capital of Malaysia, Kuala Lumpur. It offers a variety of local relaxation techniques that will relax the muscles in order to have a relaxed deep sleep. Their massages are designed to treat you with gentle strokes to cure insomnia and encourage deep sleep.Their in-house essential oil in the spa will relax the body to recover from jet lag. In the end, it will enable the body to stay healthy, smooth and balanced. The key to deep sleep is a safe, fresh body where relaxation is the best treatment.

    Location: LG1-5, Lower Ground 1, Arcoris, 10, Jalan Kiara, Mont Kiara, 50480 Kuala Lumpur
    Operating Hours: Daily, 11 am - 10 pm
    Phone: +6017- 881 3990

    Bali Ayu Spa Sanctuary

    Bali Ayu Spa Sanctuary specialises in detoxification and lymphatic treatment as well as muscle relaxation and general massage. Indulge in aromatherapy massages, body scrubs, facials, hot stone and herbal compression treatments, and more. Ideal for those looking for a peaceful retreat or couples and friends who want to share a pampering experience together. In addition to Bali aromatic oil massages, Baliayu Spa also offers traditional Thai, Shiatsu, four-hand massages as well as foot reflexology, body scrubs, facials, ear candles, pre-and post-natal treatments and many more.

    Location: 57, Jalan Bangkung, Bangsar, 59100 Kuala Lumpur, Wilayah Persekutuan Kuala Lumpur
    Operating Hours: 10 am -10 pm, Daily
    Phone: +603-2094 4557

    Manjakaki

    With 18 branches in the malls and hypermarkets across KL and Klang Valley, Manjakaki becomes one of the most accessible spas. If you need to take a break while shopping and relax, you don’t have to look any further. The spa has a wide range of services, from different types of massages to body scrubs and manicures and pedicures.

    Location: Mid Valley Megamall, Lot No.2, LG, 59, Lingkaran Syed Putra, Mid Valley City, 59200 Kuala Lumpur
    Operating Hours: 10 am- 10 pm, Daily
    Phone: 017-877 9912

    Erawan Wellness Massage

    Erawan Wellness provides relaxation in the form of a range of massages in an aromatic enclosure. Customers may request various styles of massage and target areas. Offering immersive experiences that provide a quick break from everyday stress, the wellness centre is the perfect place to visit for authentic Thai massages. Get a one-hour foot massage or a 30-minute head, neck and shoulder massage to relieve sore muscles. Masseuses here are well-trained and strong, so don't be surprised when they when they apply deep to alleviate the pain in your troubled areas.

    Location: L3-20 Nu Sentral Shopping Mall, 201 Jalan Tun Sambanthan, Brickfields, 50470 Kuala Lumpur
    Operating Hours: 10 am -10 pm, Daily
    Phone: +603-2276 5699

    Urban Retreat Spa KL

    The Urban Retreat Spa presents a haven for visitors to unwind and rejuvenate mind, body, and soul after avoiding the everyday pressures of work. It's the best place to schedule a fast massage after a little retail therapy because it's conveniently placed in a comfortable nook inside a shopping destination. Cocoon yourself away from the hectic city life and enjoy a pleasurable spa getaway at one of Urban Retreat Spa's two locations – The Curve Petaling Jaya or 1 Mont Kiara in Kuala Lumpur.

    With its facilities such as Aromatherapy Massage, Deep Tissue Massage, and Swedish Thai Massage, you get the rhythmic and soothing touch of a professionally skilled massage therapist, claiming to instantly reduce stress. Relax and rejuvenate the body in its sauna room while lounging comfortably in its lounge. Urban Retreat Spa is the perfect spa for pure indulgence, with all of its top-notch massage treatments and robust spa facilities.

    Location: No 1, Lot 15, 2nd Floor, 1 Mont Kiara, Jalan Mont Kiara, Wilayah Persekutuan, Kuala Lumpur
    Website: https://urbanretreatspa.my/
    Operating Hours: 11AM – 10PM (daily)
    Phone: 017-881 3299
